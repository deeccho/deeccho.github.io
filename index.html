About Me

Hi! My name is Diane, and this is a blog about my experiences as a Cal exchange student at the University of New South Wales. Before going abroad, I'd only been out of the country once (many years ago), and I really wanted to take advantage of the opportunity while in college to venture out of the U.S. on my own and explore another country. I ended up picking Australia, a good 7,000 miles away from home, and stayed mostly in its largest city Sydney, a sister city to my hometown San Francisco.

Hopefully this blog will inspire you to go abroad some day!
